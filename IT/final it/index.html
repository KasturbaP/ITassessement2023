This is the future of Education

The British College (also known as TBC) is an independent institution located in Trade Tower Business Center Thapathali, Kathmandu.It offers British and international qualifications. The British College offers Undergraduate and Postgraduate Programmes which are awarded by its two partner UK universities: The University of the West of England (UWE) and Leeds Beckett University (LBU).
